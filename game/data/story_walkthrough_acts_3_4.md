## ACT 3 — OBSIDIAN PATH
*Selene is dead. Nyra enters. Violence becomes doctrine.*

### Phase I: Consolidation (11 scenes)

1. **Ashes in Formation** — Aeron delivers a rehearsed eulogy. 43 of 47 believe the lie. Four LIs each contributed to the cover-up. War room after: "Who leads now?"
2. **Stay With Me** — Lyra comes seeking proof Aeron is still human. Choice: honest doubt or reassurance. Desperate comfort. First OB intimacy beat (non-erotic).
3. **Silent Table** — First command meeting without Selene. Her empty chair. Zira and Tessa argue. Choice: invoke protocols, redirect to intel, or remain silent.
4. **Who Commands Now** — Ghostline signal from quarantined Sector 9 loops "Who commands now?" — someone knows Selene is dead. Nyra foreshadowed.
5. **Echoes in Rain** — Patrol to Sector 4. Ambush by disciplined ex-Echelon. Ambush STOPS on Nyra's word: "Hold." She introduces herself.
6. **Request for Alignment** — Nyra's pitch to Phoenix. 17 soldiers, all Order Division defectors. Team fractures: Zira hostile, Noelle cautiously supportive.
7. **Terms of Order** — Nyra reorganizes command overnight. New grids, supply consolidation, comms rerouting. Aeron sides with Nyra. Lyra silently breaks.
8. **Perfect Execution** — Co-planned strike: 71 seconds, 14 Echelon dead, zero Phoenix casualties. "Too clean." / "Clean is mercy." Narrator Awareness beat.
9. **Echelon Interlude 3** — Marcus's analysts: "He's not rebelling. He's competing."
10. **Tessa Friction** — Tessa confronts Aeron: "Discipline isn't healing." **KEY CHOICE**: apologize (maintains her thread) or dismiss (she retreats — gates her erotic scene in s17).
11. **Counter-Strike** — Echelon sends 8 elites to assassinate Aeron. Nyra detects. Ambush reversal. Zero Phoenix casualties. Terrifyingly efficient.

### Phase II: Relationships Under Strain (6 scenes, locked serial order)

12. **The Oath** (NYRA FIRST EROTIC) — Fractured Echelon crest. Ritual intimacy. Steel-blue, ceremonial. Choice: accept fully or temper with pragmatism. Consent gates.
13. **Proof of Life** (LYRA DEEPENING) — Same desperation as s02, degraded. "I need to know you're still in there." Consent gates.
14. **Noelle's Calculus** — Purge authorization chain confirmed. Marcus's signature. OB Aeron: "What's the tactical value."
15. **Chain of Two** (ZIRA DEEPENING) — Zira confronts about Nyra. Not about love — about ownership. "I chose you first." Consent gates.
16. **Data and Doubt** (NOELLE FIRST EROTIC + TP NAME MECHANIC) — Contradiction logs. "You're inefficient in ways you don't acknowledge." Name Mechanic embedded. Consent gates.
17. **Count the Cost** (TESSA FIRST EROTIC, CONDITIONAL) — Only fires if player apologized in s10. Quietest OB scene. Consent gates.

### Phase III: Mirror & Rejection (9 scenes, with interstitial LI beats)

18. **Field Sync** — Bloodline resonance, degraded. Quarter-beat phase difference. Cold calibration.
19. **The Weight** — Tessa alone at the board. Selene's chair empty. Counting names.
20. *[NEW] Zira: Before the Weight* — Solo interior. Board overwrite. "Nobody thought OPERATIONAL was worth stopping for." The pressed flower.
21. **The Letter** — Zira finds Liora's letter. "I fell in love with the pause." OB Aeron: "The pause is what gets people killed."
22. **You Don't Get to Break** — Zira confronts Aeron about what he's becoming. Catalogs the changes: Nyra's language in his mouth, Lyra sidelined.
23. *[NEW] Lyra: Prays for the Hunt* — First outgoing blessing. Blessing operators going to kill. "I am not stopping it. I am being in the room with it."
24. **The Story Keeper** — Zira traces courier patterns. Liora is alive. She left. She abandoned him.
25. **The Hunt** — Echelon deploys 1,200 personnel. Aeron orchestrates a masterpiece: Noelle's models + Zira's false feeds + Nyra's soldiers. Echelon sweeps empty buildings.
26. *[NEW] Nyra: Before the Mirror* — Post-hunt. "You are not your father. You are something your father did not predict."
27. **Bookend Before the Mirror** — Group meal. Same room as EMP's bookend. Everything that made EMP feel like home is missing. Maps not cleared. No herbs.
28. *[NEW] Noelle: Before Liora* — 73% probability of refusal. "I am telling you this because withholding painful data is cruelty I will not practice."
29. **LIORA** (ACT 3 OB FINALE) — Aeron meets his mother at a neutral safe house. She looks at him like Marcus in a different uniform. She refuses absolution. She turns away and walks.
30. **Aftermath** — Nyra: "She chose weakness." Zira: silent. Tessa: "She's right." Lyra: prays. → Act 4.

## ACT 3 — EMPATHY PATH
*Selene alive. Shared command. Compassion tested under fire.*

### Phase I: Proving Ground (11 scenes)

1. **Back from Edge** — Selene and Aeron co-lead publicly for the first time. Supply run planned.
2. **Breath of Faith** (LYRA KISS GATE) — Lyra finds Aeron struggling. Breathing exercise. Can escalate to first tender intimate (gated on trust/affection). Consent choice.
3. **Shared Ground** — Supply interdiction. 14-second window. Co-plan with Selene. First successful joint op.
4. **The Silence** (TP MECHANIC) — Council meeting. 200 civilians need extraction. Four options labeled as others' frameworks (Marcus/Kael/Selene/Noelle). 20-second timer → Aeron can refuse all borrowed voices. True Path seed.
5. **Two Healers** — Lyra + Tessa co-treat casualties. Medicine vs ritual. "Discipline serves care." Poly nudge.
6. **Woman from Sector 7** — Lyra meets Hana, whose daughter she "relocated" in a sweep. Choice: confess partially or hold cover.
7. **Orders and Prayers** — Lyra and Selene clash over civilian proximity zone. Setting guardrails for joint decisions.
8. **Echelon Strikes Back** — Marcus counter-strikes while Aeron is away. Surgical. "I wrote patrol routes like this." He recognizes his own handwriting. Choice embedded.
9. **The Unmeasurable** — Lyra and Noelle: breathing exercise as test. "The Unmeasurable" logged. Cross-pair anchor.
10. **Echelon Interlude 3** — Logistics officer presents purge window. "They're children."
11. **Field Sync** — Bloodline resonance under fire. Hearts sync. Bands hum. Combat buffs.

### Phase II: Deepening (5 scenes — Noelle, Zira, Lyra stay; Tessa + Selene moved to Phase III)

12. **Empirical Tenderness** (NOELLE FIRST EROTIC + TP NAME MECHANIC) — "No data between us." Name Mechanic: invisible text field. If player types "Aeron" → menu dissolves, intimate registers differently. Consent gates. "I have no unit for this."
13. **Corridor Op** — 200 civilians extracted through Echelon sweep gaps. Peak competency. Narrator Awareness beat.
14. **Cipher and Skin** (SELENE FIRST KISS) — Late night ops table. The cipher becomes something personal. First kiss. Not erotic yet — the buildup.
15. **Signal Under Fire** (ZIRA DEEPENING) — Joint op. Adrenaline → banter → real. Consent gates.
16. **Faith in Flaws** (LYRA DEEPENING) — Observation deck. The Unmeasurable / Aneva. "The divine moves through broken vessels." Consent gates.

### Phase III: Revelation & Cost (12 scenes, intimate beats interleaved with plot)

17. **The Weight** — Tessa + Selene pair anchor. Counting the living vs counting the dead.
18. **The Letter** — Zira finds Liora's letter. "I fell in love with the pause." EMP Aeron softens. The pause connects to the Emergence Mechanic.
19. *[NEW] Zira: The Relay* — Zira processes delivering the letter. "I found her for you. I don't know if that was a gift or a weapon."
20. **Noelle Revelation** — Purge data confirmed. Marcus's signature on the kill order. Proof of orchestrated mass murder. Choice: how to use the proof.
21. **Command and Surrender** (SELENE FIRST EROTIC) — Post-revelation vulnerability. "Don't let this change how we lead." / "It already has." Consent gates.
22. **The Story Keeper** — Zira finds the courier pattern. "A woman who keeps the names." Aeron realizes: his mother is alive.
23. **Scar and Steady** (TESSA FIRST EROTIC) — Tessa treats accumulated wounds after the revelation. Her hands on his injuries. The Brand scar. "You're still here." Consent gates.
24. *[NEW] Lyra: Archive* — Lyra recognizes Liora's handwriting from Cathedral registries. "I have seen this hand before."
25. **Bookend Before the Storm** — All five LIs together. Warm. Poly foreshadowing. Callbacks: balcony rail, Kael's rail, Tier Hall. "This is home."
26. **LIORA EXECUTION** (ACT 3 EMP FINALE) — Captured through courier network Aeron's compassion exposed. Public execution in the Aeries. Marcus present. Her last act: she says a name. The cost of compassion made flesh.
27. *[NEW] Tessa: After Liora* — Tessa writes LIORA RYLAN on both columns. Rule of three. "She kept six thousand names. I keep a board with two columns."
28. **Aftermath** — Each LI responds. Selene: "Marcus just made this personal for everyone." Zira: running ops. "We don't stop. We finish this." → Act 4.

## ACT 4 — OBSIDIAN PATH: Violence Normalized
*The regime establishes itself around Selene's murder and Liora's refusal.*

### Phase I: Regime Consolidation (7 scenes)

1. **The Cold Room** — Morning after Liora refused. Aeron locked in the strategic room. Nyra waits 20 minutes without knocking again. One mark on the plan. ACT IV title card.
2. **The New Shape** — Nyra formally restructures Phoenix command. Hierarchical tree: Aeron/Nyra at apex. "This is not a new room. This is the room we have been in since she died."
3. **Marcus Adapts** — Same intel as EMP (Rhea Vestin incoming, 6-day clock). OB response: "We do not give him six days." Preemptive strike authorized. No one objects.
4. **Lyra Prays Alone** — Band cinched past comfort. Prayer won't hold. Aeries diction as paperwork. "If I leave, I release him to Nyra."
5. **Zira Cannot Leave** — Go-bag packed for fire, not flight. Pressed flower on the bench. Solo interior. She is not choosing him again. She is choosing to stop pretending she has a choice.
6. **Tessa at the Board** — LIVING overwritten to OPERATIONAL on adhesive plastic strip. Tessa leaves her clip lamp in the room. Aeron wipes Liora from the LIVING column with his sleeve.
7. **Aeron and Nyra: Cold** — "If she had stayed, would I have stopped?" Nyra: "Yes. Briefly. Then you would have resumed and hated her for having made you stop." He asks the question only she can answer.

### Phase II: Normalization + Dark Intimacy (12 scenes)

8. **Echelon Interlude 4** — Marcus: "He is building something I understand." Disappointed. Rhea: "I do not require a briefing. I require the window."
9. **Sector 12 Sweep** — Preemptive strike. 22 minutes, zero Phoenix casualties, 17 Echelon dead, 3 civilians on the target list Aeron signed. "Clean."
10. **Noelle's Doctrine** — Noelle drafting civilian targeting framework without being asked. Aeron signs it BLANK — before it's finished. "This is how it happens." / "I know."
11. **Second Oath** (NYRA EROTIC DEEPENING) — Ceremonial sealing. "Nyra is not a name tonight. She is a temperature." Consent gates (3 OB modes). Cold aftercare.
12. **Zira Confrontation** — "Make me feel it." She demands terms. Formal seat at the tactical table.
13. **Zira Deepening** (EROTIC) — Anger converted to heat. The pressed flower stays hers. Consent gates.
14. **Lyra Sanctifying Violence** — Blesses the outgoing operators for the first time. "I am not stopping it. I am being in the room with it."
15. *[NEW] Oath and Flower* (POLY: NYRA + ZIRA + AERON) — Accidental collision. Ceremony vs pressed flower. "I do not share command. But tonight I am not commanding." Poly-gated consent.
16. **Lyra Deepening** (EROTIC) — "Body as the last place I can pray honestly." She unbuckles the Band — surrender, not sacrament. Rebuckles before leaving.
17. **Tessa Fracture Point** — Conditional: peels the OPERATIONAL strip off. Stay-and-resist OR stay-but-withdraw. Hard branch.
18. **Tessa Conditional Beat** — Branch A: grief-sex of remembering OR Branch B: doorway turned away from. Shadow under the door.

### Phase III: Rhea + the Letter + the Turn (10 scenes)

19. **Rhea Arrives** — 7 Phoenix dead including Siri Ondel (Tessa's trainee, 19). Rhea's method: studies how Phoenix thinks and cuts at the seams.
20. **Tessa: The Trainee** — Conditional: "She was nineteen" (write on LIVING, not DEAD) OR "I'm not going to touch the board."
21. **Noelle Deepening** (EROTIC) — "A hand I cannot predict." Weapon declaration. "Close the door on your way out."
22. *[NEW] Doctrine and Weapon* (POLY: NYRA + NOELLE + AERON) — "We are both here for the same man and different reasons. That is the first honest data point." Poly-gated consent.
23. **The Letter** — Liora's letter: "I fell in love with the pause." OB keeps it as gravestone, not door. "Six thousand names. The Ghostline is her ghost."
24. **Council of Attrition** — Full council votes to assassinate Rhea Vestin personally in 7 days. Each LI contributes. Lyra blesses the operator. Zira runs ground. Noelle predicts. Nyra holds the ritual. Tessa (Branch A): "I will receive the wounded."
25. **Aeron Alone** — Solo mirror scene. Full inventory of his violence. The Glass callback. "The mask is the face." Returns to the HEAD chair. OB mirror of EMP's side chair.
26. **Nyra: The Mirror** — Doctrinal seduction. She reframes the letter: "Your mother was wrong empirically." Aeron refuses HALFWAY — keeps letter as gravestone, not refutation. "You are smarter than I expected."
27. **Lyra at the Altar** — Asks the Six to count the assassination against HER, not Aeron. "I cannot let it be counted against you."
28. **ACT IV CLOSE** — Dark 5-LI relay. Zira: soldier's acknowledgment. Noelle: OP-203 (operation code, not "version two"). Tessa (Branch A): the lamp. Lyra: Band gesture. Nyra: "Chapter four. We did not flinch."

## ACT 4 — EMPATHY PATH: Shared Authority
*Aeron learns to lead without becoming singular. Thesis: "Delegate the question."*

### Phase I: Learning to Lead (7 scenes)

1. **Morning After Broadcast** — Tone scene. Aeron walks the base alone at dawn. ACT IV title card. "Today we find out what we actually built."
2. **First Cracks** — Council scene. Under pressure, Aeron reverts to singular command. Selene publicly challenges. **KEY CHOICE**: accept correction (EMP) or defend instinct (OB-factor). Accountability promise invoked.
3. **Marcus Adapts** — Noelle presents 12-36 hour lag pattern. Rhea Vestin incoming. 6-day clock. "The student has taught the teacher."
4. **Lyra Unbuckled** — Band removed first time in 11 years. Old Tongue word "Anayet" spoken. Ritualized re-buckling.
5. **Delegation Test** — Major op where Aeron commanded NOT to lead. Lyra leads, makes different calls. KIA: Kesa Marin, Joren Hale. Selene's Kael-at-24 confession begins.
6. **You Are Not a Machine** — Tessa forces rest after 38 hours. "Your body is not a resource to be spent." Rule of three applied to a living person: "Aeron. Aeron. Aeron."
7. **Tessa Counts Different** — Tessa adds Kesa and Joren to the board SILENTLY. Aeron says them three times; Tessa knows because "the silence changes shape." LIORA RYLAN on both columns.

### Phase II: The Weight of Shared Command (12 scenes)

8. **Echelon Interlude 4** — Marcus empty-chair soliloquy. Rhea Vestin planted.
9. **Zira's Exit Plan** — She built forged papers, a safehouse chain, a route out of Solveil. Only his name on it. "I needed to know the door existed."
10. **Noelle Implicated** — Her predictive framework WORKS but requires her to behave as strategist, not analyst. She asks permission to stop pretending. Signs the framework. First time she's signed a document.
11. **Zira Calls Him Out** — "You sat alone in the mess with your coffee and took Kesa Marin's name like it was a private tax." She demands to be let INTO his grief.
12. **What Selene Meant** (THESIS CENTER) — Selene finishes the Kael story. "You are still trying to delegate tasks. I am asking you to delegate the question." Shared command ≠ distributing weight. It means the weight BELONGS to more than one person.
13. **Mercy Call** — 6 captured Echelon soldiers. Standard rebel doctrine: execute the lifer. Aeron refuses. Holds all six. 38% compromise risk in 21 days. "Mercy is a decision made today."
14. **Prayer After Mercy** — Lyra introduces "Seren": to hold what will break you. "The Six do not grade mercy. They count it."
15. **The Ones He Lost** — Aeron alone with the full KIA list. Rule of three. Gets through four clusters. Breaks on Liora Rylan. Op 391 callback. Selene finds him, says nothing, borrows his coffee.
16. **Held Not Fixed** — Tessa: "I cannot fix this. I am not going to try. But you are held. That is not nothing."
17. **She Is Not Just Tactical** — Selene's letter from her sister Ilene (unreachable). Aeron's hand on the back of her chair, not her shoulder. She is not a doctrine delivery mechanism.
18. **Protecting Exhaustion** — Council forces 48-hour stand-down on Aeron. Noelle's framework predicts breakdown in 60 hours. Zira uses the door line: "This is the door. Open it for 48 hours."
19. **Quiet After Failure** — Noelle says "I love you" for the first time. As data. "I do not know how to weight it." Two people on the floor, not speaking.

### Phase III: Climax — Individual Deepenings + Poly Beats (11 scenes)

20. **Return to the Table** — Aeron takes the SIDE chair, not the head. Nobody comments. Everyone notices. Joran Tev walking the corridor. Kael's "person first" echo.
21. *[NEW] Familiar Ground* (POLY: LYRA + ZIRA + AERON) — Mapping station, late night. "Is this what we are doing? All three of us?" Poly-gated consent.
22. **Zira Deepening** (EROTIC) — Backward-running clock. "Did you come to use the door, or to stay?" Consent gates.
23. **Rhea Strikes** — Davel Ostra KIA. Rhea's method: precision cuts shaped around Noelle's framework. The framework is now a vulnerability. "Take twelve."
24. *[NEW] Door and Data* (POLY: ZIRA + NOELLE + AERON) — Post-Rhea recalibration. "Then stop modeling." Poly-gated consent.
25. **Selene Deepening** (EROTIC) — The letter face-down. "I don't know how to be held by a student." Consent gates.
26. **Noelle Deepening** (EROTIC) — "I said it as a variable before. I am saying it as a sentence now." Consent gates.
27. **Lyra Deepening** (EROTIC) — Band off tonight. "I have prayed to be allowed to be a woman and not only a vessel." Consent gates.
28. **Tessa Deepening** (EROTIC) — The jacket folded. "Let me be not-useful." Consent gates.
29. *[NEW] Command and the Door* (POLY: SELENE + ZIRA + AERON) — "I delegate the question to both of you tonight." Poly-gated consent.
30. **Marcus Second Cut** — Rhea delivers "SOON" list of Phoenix real names. Council decides to move the civilian network. All five at the table, no one at the head.
31. **ACT IV CLOSE** — Five-LI relay at the base edge. Zira: "Door." Noelle: "Version two." Lyra: "Seren." Tessa: "You are held." Selene: "Chapter four. We did not break." Aeron internalizes the mercy mantra. Backward clock callback.
